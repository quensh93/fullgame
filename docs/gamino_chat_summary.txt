
Project Name: Gamino

Product Overview:
Gamino is an online multiplayer mobile app offering popular Iranian and global card and board games. It provides both team-based and individual formats, delivering an engaging, competitive, and fair gaming experience.

Target Audience:
Users aged 18 to 65, including Iranian and international users who enjoy online gaming.

Supported Games and Rules:

1. Hokm (4 players):
- Team-based (2 vs 2), 52 cards.
- Modes: Normal, NARAS, SARAS, TAK_NARAS.
- First Ace receiver is the ruler.
- Trump rules and scoring defined explicitly.

2. Shelem (4 players):
- Team-based (2 vs 2), 52 cards.
- Card distribution: 12 cards each player, 4 leftover cards.
- Bidding starts from 105 to 165 in increments of 5.
- Special "Shelem" bid doubles risk and reward.
- Game ends when a team reaches 1165 or the predefined score.

3. Hearts (Bi-Del, 4 players):
- Individual gameplay, 52 cards.
- No trump.
- Negative scoring: each heart card = 1 negative, Queen of Spades = 13 negative.
- Total negative points: 26.
- Game ends at a predefined negative score (50, 70, 100).
- Winner has the lowest negative score.

4. Haft Khabis (4 to 7 players):
- Individual gameplay, 52 or 104 cards based on players.
- Special card rules defined explicitly (8, Jack, Ace, 10, 2, 7).
- First to discard all cards wins.

5. Backgammon (2 players):
- Traditional gameplay.
- Adjustable winning rounds (3, 5, 7).
- Special rules: players can't continue moving the same piece after capturing in their own home.

6. Rock-Paper-Scissors:
- Quick, casual gameplay.
- Adjustable winning rounds (3, 5, 7).

Application Features:
- User registration (email-based), referral codes.
- User profile management (personal info, wallet addresses).
- Coin economy: initial free coins, game entry fees (fixed amounts: 50, 100, 250, 500, 1000 coins).
- System fee: 10% of total room entry.
- Withdrawal of coins through cryptocurrency (USDT).
- Referral rewards.
- Game rooms: public and private (with invite codes).
- Social interactions: friends list, block users, limited chat.
- Notifications, tutorials, spectator mode.
- Anti-cheating measures.

Technical Details:
- Backend: Spring Boot, JWT authentication.
- Frontend: Flutter with Riverpod (state management), GoRouter (routing), Dio (network), Hive & SharedPreferences (local storage), get_it (dependency injection).
- Multi-language support (English, Persian).
- UI based on Material Design.

Revenue Model:
- Entry fee revenue (10% system commission).
- In-app purchases (coin packs).
- Referral rewards system.

Competitive Advantages:
- Variety of games.
- Comprehensive social and anti-cheat systems.
- Scalable and modular architecture.

Future Roadmap:
- Player rankings and leagues.
- Periodic tournaments.
- Customizable avatars and badges.
- Enhanced AI and recommendations.

Additional notes:
- XP and coin penalties for intentional game abandonment and disconnects.
- Games continue until predefined negative points are reached for Hearts (Bi-Del).
